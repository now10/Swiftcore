Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Apple Developer Accounts

What's the difference between pre-approved and new accounts?

Pre-approved accounts are already created, verified, and ready for immediate use. New accounts require the standard Apple application process, which we handle on your behalf. Pre-approved accounts are delivered within 24-48 hours, while new accounts take 3-14 days depending on type and region.

Is it legal to purchase a pre-approved Apple Developer Account?

Yes, it's legal to transfer ownership of an Apple Developer Account. The original account owner legally transfers all rights and ownership to you. We provide full documentation of the transfer process. All accounts are created following Apple's Terms of Service.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via USDT TRC20 only. This ensures fast, secure, and anonymous transactions. We do not accept credit cards, PayPal, or bank transfers due to the nature of these services and to protect both buyer and seller privacy.

How long does delivery take after payment?

Pre-approved accounts: 24-48 hours after payment confirmation. New Individual accounts: 3-7 business days. New Organization accounts: 7-14 business days. Enterprise accounts: 14-21 business days. Delivery times may vary based on Apple's verification processes.

What happens after the account expires?

All Apple Developer Accounts require annual renewal. We provide renewal services at a discounted rate. You'll receive renewal reminders 30 days before expiration. Failure to renew will result in loss of access to developer tools and removal of apps from the App Store.
